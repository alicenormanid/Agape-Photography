About Louisa

Louisa believes that taking your photos should be a fun, comfortable, and easygoing experience! She is a portrait, wedding, and special event photographer primarily located in Edmonton, Alberta, but able to travel province-wide.

Her Passion

Portrait photography has been a favourite pastime of Louisa's since the age of 14. Louisa has done countless fun photoshoots with her friends and peers throughout junior high, high school, and university. Her favourite part of taking pictures is getting to hang out with new people and make them feel good with the final result.
